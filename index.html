  • Drove churn reason attribution coverage to 90%+ of accounts, more than doubling existing Salesforce tracked churn visibility.
  • Improved churn data integrity by eliminating duplicate and inconsistent reason lost codes within Salesforce and creating a full data lineage model to trace, validate, and govern upstream churn attribution sources.

MA Scorecard Agent

An end-to-end automated pipeline that turns a Teams message into a branded HTML presentation through a single prompt.

  • Reduced company wide scorecard update cycles from 2/3 hours of manual slide editing to under 5 minutes, enabling live dashboard refreshes as roadmap priorities shift in real time for the C-suite audience.
  • Replaced inconsistent roadmap decks across 5 senior teams with a single living dashboard, freeing up meaningful hours of executive time previously spent on formatting and version control.
  • Currently live with approximately 15 senior stakeholders, with a clear path to scale across 100+ members of Moody's senior management. Extensible to general presentations and lightweight data dashboards beyond scorecards.
